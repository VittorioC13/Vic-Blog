God's Plan

why children are always innovative?

why children are always curious?

why children can see things we can't?

why children are free and happy?

because they are empty.

God always see room inside children.

they don't plan nothing for the future.

they don't strategize their life.

they leave the room completely for God's plan.

it all comes from being 'empty'.

the greatest of all time never know themselves will be.

they just follow god's plan.
