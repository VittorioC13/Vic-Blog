Lao Tzu's startup lesson - 2

in Dao De Jing by Lao Tzu - chapter 7

the world is oftentimes work reversely.

what you give attention the most often will be the first ditched by the world.

以其不自生，故能长生

it means you need to build without thinking about self-interest to really gain personal success and longevity and sustainability.

...后其身而身先，外其身而身存

it alerts this AI age, that only if you don't chase to be "First Mover", you will be the first mover. only if you don't crave to stay, you actually stay.
