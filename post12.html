Zen & Startup

as i continue to read, feel, think Zen.

i found commonality between startup and Zen.

1)

imagine Zen as an exam.

even if you are handed the exact answer, you still can't enlighten.

so do startups.

even if we have millions of successful founder stories and advice

you still can't win until you really work on your own path.

2)

Zen is not about shaving head and become monks.

Zen is about contemplate while you are.

you can get infinite inspiration through life

and in one moment you will be enlightened and enter the zone.

so do startups.

you can't just always iterate iterate iterate.

you gotta step outside and really live a life.

and then some part of life will sharpen your intuition and makes you unstoppable.
